About Me

I’m an analytical and outcome-oriented Product Manager with 4.5 years of experience, beginning my career in data science before transitioning into product management after completing a certification from ISB. My journey from analytics to product has given me a strong foundation in turning insights into user-focused, scalable solutions.

I’ve led initiatives spanning conversational AI, data-driven growth, and predictive analytics—designing solutions that improved product performance, reduced costs, and enhanced user engagement. Experienced in roadmapping, user research, and go-to-market strategies, I collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to align product vision with business goals.

Adept at bridging technical and non-technical stakeholders, I bring a blend of technical expertise and strategic thinking to build products from 0 to 1 with vision, and scale them from 1 to 100 through execution and iteration.
